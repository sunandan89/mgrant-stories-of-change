Sunita Devi, a 34-year-old mother of two from Dharampur village, was among the first cohort of women to join our Self-Help Group programme in January 2025.


Before the programme, Sunita's family survived on seasonal agricultural income of approximately ₹4,000 per month. Through the SHG, she received micro-enterprise training and a seed loan of ₹15,000 to start a small tailoring business...
